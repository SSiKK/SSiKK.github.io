Round 1

Initial Structuring of Design

I went through several design iterations for the main structuring of the components accessible to the user. I divided the available components into static and dynamic parts. The static elements being the "always displayed" elements on the door in the form of the menu system, available widgets and notifications. The dynamic elements of the screen are the options that are changing depending on the options selected on the menu system.
